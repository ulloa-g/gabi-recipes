Barbecue

This is a delicios dish to prepare for your friends on a sunday and have a great time together!. This could be perfectly for lunch or just dinner if you wish

Let's start cooking!

Time spent

  • Prep time: 15mins
  • Cook time: 1hr 30mins
  • Total time: 1hr 45mins

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Chorizo (spicy pork sausage)
  • Blood sausage
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Set up the fire
  2. Salt the beef and add a little of pepper (chorizos and blood sausage are not neccesary)
  3. Once the ember are ready you can add the beef on top of the grill
